Benny the Bunny is hungry, but hungry for apple pie!

There's an apple tree in the woods with apples falling all the time, what a waste!

Benny decided to grab his fruits basket and collect them to bake his precious apple pie

Instructions

Move the basket to collect the apples

Try to collect as many apples as possible before the time is up!

Beware!!

There's a witch who doesn't like Benny the Bunny and will try to poison him by putting poisoned apples along with the normal ones.

Avoid catching them or the game will end!
